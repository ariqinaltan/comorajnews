Eva Longoria Uses This Hyaluronic Acid Eye Serum

Celebrities, they’re just like us — at least when it comes to the skincare ingredients they swear by. Eva Longoria, for example, loves one of the best-known, dermatologist-acclaimed skin hydrators, hyaluronic acid (HA). The actress professed her love for the skin quenching ingredient in a recent PopSugar interview, sharing, “I live and die by hyaluronic acid.” 

Incidentally, she mentioned her fondness for a specific skincare line packed with HA: L’Oréal’s Revitalift line. The ever-glowing Longoria, a spokesperson for the brand since 2005 (and among one of the glowiest celebrities since the second she stepped into fame, TBH) loves the Revitalift Eye Serum, which she says “feels delicious to apply.” 

Shop now: $33; amazon.com

The L'Oréal Paris Revitalift Hyaluronic Acid and Caffeine Eye Serum is a cooling, quenching under-eye serum powered by hyaluronic acid. In addition to HA, the serum features caffeine as a key ingredient thanks to its tightening and depuffing effects, both of which enliven tired-looking under eyes. “Caffeine constricts blood vessels, which increases circulation and acts as an anti-inflammatory,” board-certified dermatologist and founder of Skin Wellness Dermatology Corey L. Hartman, M.D., previously told InStyle.

Longoria cited the product’s cooling rollerball applicator as a primary factor in its sensorially scrumptious feel. Aside from the refreshing feeling of a cold trail of serum across the undereyes, the metal applicator is functional, too. The material retains coldness, which helps to reduce puffiness under the eyes and elsewhere. No wonder Longoria calls the rollerball applicator “amazing.”

More than 2,700 Amazon shoppers have left the serum a five-star review, raving that it’s quenching, soothing, and incredibly hydrating. One shopper said the serum “really works” and claimed that their eyes looked “younger” thanks to it. Another shopper echoed a similar sentiment, noting that “the product does what it says it does,” and “made a big difference” after six months of use. 

To experience the eye serum that Eva Longoria calls “so delicious,” shop the L’Oréal Revitalift Eye Serum at Amazon for a skin-soothing (and smoothing) treat.
