Wigmaker Drops Detailed Video Of Sasha Banks Wild NJPW Wrestle Kingdom Hair

Sasha Banks became free agent after her WWE contract expired on New Year’s Eve. The multiple-time WWE Women’s Champion recently made her highly anticipated entry at the Wrestle Kingdom 17 event as Mercedes Mone. With that being said, Banks also debuted new look at the event with a new wig, and the wig maker recently released a detailed video of it.

Mercedes Mone has a history of switching her hairdos according to situations, but the new one is really wild. Banks’ wigmaker, Shannon Romano, recently took to his Instagram to drop a detailed video of her new wild hair and also thanked her for the opportunity.

“Bank on Mone’ #njpw #stardom @themercedesvarnado thank you so much for the opportunity!! @p_i_g_m_e_n_t wigzzz #pigmentwigzzz #firewig #MercedesMone’ #artinmotion

Wig designer/artist- @p_i_g_m_e_n_t
Hair- @vpfashionhairpro
Color- @manicpanicnyc #manicpanic”

Mercedes Mone’s new hair is orange with blue, black, and red accents that resemble blazing flames. Mercedes entered the event wearing stunning attire that gave her the appearance of an Egyptian queen.

The former Sasha Banks showed up to confront KAIRI following her successful IWGP Women’s Championship defense against Tam Nakano. Stay tuned to Ringside News for more updates regarding the pro-wrestling industry.
