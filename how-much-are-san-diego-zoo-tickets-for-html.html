How Much Are San Diego Zoo Tickets For Military

Military Mission: Conquering the San Diego Zoo on a Budget

So, you're a brave soldier (or sailor, or airman, or... you get the idea) stationed in San Diego, and you're itching for an adventure that doesn't involve camouflage and KP duty. The San Diego Zoo is calling your name, with its furry, feathery, and sometimes-scaly residents. But before you suit up for your next expedition (khaki cargo pants don't count!), let's crack the code on those zoo tickets.

Operation Free Entry: Active Duty Gets a Salute

First things first, active-duty military personnel get a whopping 100% discount (that's military speak for FREE!). Just show your valid ID at the gate, and those lions, tigers, and bears (oh my!) will be lining up to welcome you. Consider it a small token of appreciation for your service (and maybe a way to keep you away from the penguins... those little guys can be troublemakers).

Dependents Deploy for a Discount

Military spouses and dependents, fear not! You may not get a full pardon from park fees, but you do snag a 10% discount on a 1-Day Pass. Just flash that dependent ID card and get ready to explore. Think of it as your reward for putting up with all those early mornings and deployments (and maybe for convincing your significant other that khaki cargo pants are a fashion statement).

Important Note: This discount applies to both the San Diego Zoo and the San Diego Zoo Safari Park, so you can choose your animal adventure.

Intel on Regular Ticket Prices (Just in Case)

Now, for our civilian friends tagging along (or those sneaky soldiers trying to sneak in a plus one), here's a quick rundown on regular ticket prices:

  • Adult (ages 12+): $74 (ouch!)
  • Child (ages 3-11): $64 (slightly less ouchy)

There are also some multi-day and package deals available, but those are classified information for another briefing.

Frequently Asked Questions: Your Mission Debriefing

How to snag that military discount? Easy! Just show your valid military ID (active duty) or dependent ID card at the ticket window.

How much does it cost for my family to visit? That depends on your family size and if anyone qualifies for the military discount. Check out the zoo's website for current pricing on regular tickets https://zoo.sandiegozoo.org/tickets

Is there a discount for veterans? Unfortunately, there is no specific discount for veterans at this time.

Can I buy tickets in advance? Absolutely! You can purchase tickets online or at the gate.

What should I wear to the zoo? Comfort is key! Think breathable clothing and shoes you can walk in all day. Leave the camouflage at home, though – you might blend in with the zebras!

So there you have it, soldier! With this intel, you're all set to conquer the San Diego Zoo and have an unforgettable adventure. Just remember, don't try to pet the lions (no matter how cute they look), and maybe lay off the peanuts – those monkeys are notorious for snatching snacks.
