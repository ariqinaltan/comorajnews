The Yorkshire Vet | PBS

Follow James Herriot’s real-life veterinary practice in his picturesque town in Yorkshire.MoreMore

In The Yorkshire Vet, Julian Norton is joined by his partner, fellow vet and former Herriot trainee, Peter Wright, as well as their dedicated team of staff at Skeldale Veterinary Centre (James Herriot’s original practice). Viewers get an inside look at the practice, based in the picturesque market town of Thirsk, North Yorkshire, and its clientele – both two-legged and four-legged!
