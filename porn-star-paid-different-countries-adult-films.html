How much do porn stars get paid?

Generally, male performers earn less than female actresses in the porn industry.

It's hard to generalise how much porn actors get paid since there aren't many regulations, and there is a large discrepancy between those starting their careers and the well-established adult stars.

Those behind the raunchy scenes aren't paid nearly as well as their counterparts in Hollywood, despite the industry's multi-billion pound price tag.

That's according to founder of LA Direct Models, Derek Hay, a man who represents many top adult performers and used to work as an actor himself.

Derek added: "I don’t think that adult stars make as much money as the general public perceives them to make.

Who are the richest porn stars?

According to Celebritynetworth.com, the highest paid female porn star is Sunny Leone - who is also the richest adult film actress in the world.

As well as having a networth of more than a whopping $16million - roughly £12million - she has also built a loyal fanbase on Instagram, boasting close to 55million followers.

As for the men, Johnny Sins, born in Pennsylvania, reportedly comes out on top with an estimated net worth of five million dollars.

Do porn stars earn more in other countries?

While size might not always matter, thee producer's location does.

Porn stars earn less in Eastern Europe and Russia than those working in LA or Western Europe, such as France, Germany and Italy.

There are also laws in different countries surrounding the minimum age, and what sort of content is allowed to be produced, so if one prohibits certain scenes, the money made from the film itself may be lower.

How do you become an adult movie star?

According to Buttercamlord, there a number of adult production companies that are seeking models and actors for their content - both photo and video.

You usually get paid for your initial shoot, which will then be published on a website to get your name (or more importantly, body) out there.

That exposure will boost your name in the industry, and nowadays many porn stars take to social media to boost their profile, as a more famous name will drag in more viewers, making them attractive to producers.
