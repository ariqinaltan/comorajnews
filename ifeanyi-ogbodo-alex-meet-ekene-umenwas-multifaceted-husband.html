Early Life & Education

Ifeanyi Ogbodo Alex, also known as Alex Kleanson, a Nigerian citizen, was born on November 20, 1990, in Enugu State, Nigeria. 

Throughout his upbringing, he was under the loving care of his parents, whose names remain undisclosed to the public. However, one may refer to his parents as Mr. and Mrs. Ogbodo.

Ifeanyi Ogbodo Alex grew up with his parents in Enugu State, where he completed his early education. He attended Caritas University in Enugu, earning a bachelor’s degree in computer science.

Additionally, he holds a Master’s degree in cybersecurity from the University of Liverpool. Alex has three siblings named Jude Ogbodo, Nd Ogbodo, and Uchechukwu Ogbodo Nwatu.

Career

Ifeanyi Ogbodo Alex, also known as Alex Kleanson, embarked on his professional journey as a manager at NELTV. Still, later on, he ventured into the world of Nollywood Picture TV, where he assumed the roles of both Webmaster and Senior Information Technology Manager. 

Presently, Ifeanyi Ogbodo Alex holds the esteemed position of General manager at Firstnolly TV while simultaneously being the CEO of Kleanson Concept. His remarkable film production company has been responsible for creating an impressive catalog of over 200 highly acclaimed Nollywood movies, such as Female Police, Black Widows, Death Wish, Queen of The Night, The Arrival, Money is Power, and many more captivating titles.

Alex Kleanson, a highly experienced individual in the industry, has had the privilege of collaborating with numerous renowned Nollywood celebrities. Some of these stars are Ekene Umenwa, Ken Erics, Mercy Johnson, Yul Edochie, Stephen Odimgbe, Flashboy, Okaro Onyiuke, Eve Esin, Jerry Williams, Destiny Etiko, and Zubby Michael.

With his extensive portfolio of working alongside such accomplished talents, Ifeanyi Ogbodo Alex‘s expertise and contributions to the Nigerian film industry are undeniable.
