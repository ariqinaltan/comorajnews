Joe Joyce Net Worth 2023

Joe Joyce is a 37-year-old professional boxer from England who is a former WBO interim heavyweight champion. Starting his professional career in 2017, Joyce's boxing record is 15 wins and 1 loss.

Over the years, Joyce has earned attention through his 14 knockout wins, and that too against some of the most prominent names in the division, like Joseph Parker, Daniel Dubois, Carlos Takam, Christian Hammer, etc.

Joe Joyce is set to face Zhilei Zhang for a WBO interim heavyweight title rematch on September 23, 2023, in Wembley, London.
