Who was Hostage Elia Toledano? Wiki, Biography, Age, Family, Girlfriend, Obituary, Death &amp; More

Elia Toledano was a 28-year-old French-Israeli man who was tragically killed during the October 7 rave massacre carried out by Hamas terrorists in Gaza. He was one of the two women who were freed by Hamas after 55 days of captivity on December 14, 2023. His friend Mia Schem was also released by the terror group.

Wiki

Elia Toledano was born on June 15, 1995 in Paris, France. He had dual citizenship of France and Israel. He studied computer science at the École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL) in Switzerland and graduated in 2017. He then worked as a software engineer at a startup company in Tel Aviv, Israel.

Biography

Toledano loved music and technology. He was passionate about creating innovative solutions for social problems using artificial intelligence and blockchain. He also enjoyed traveling and exploring new cultures. He had visited more than 20 countries in Europe, Asia, Africa and America.

On October 7, 2023, he decided to attend the Nova festival in the Israeli desert with his friend Mia Schem. The Nova festival is an annual event that showcases cutting-edge technology and creativity from around the world. It attracts thousands of attendees who enjoy music, art, games and workshops.

However, their trip turned into a nightmare when they were ambushed by Hamas militants who stormed into the festival grounds. The terrorists opened fire indiscriminately at the crowd of people who were enjoying themselves. Elia tried to protect Mia Schem but he was shot multiple times by one of the attackers.

Mia Schem managed to escape from the scene and contacted her family in France. She told them what happened and asked them to help her rescue Toledano from Gaza. She also posted a video on social media where she pleaded with Hamas to release him alive.
