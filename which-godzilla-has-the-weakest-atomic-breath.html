Can Godzilla be hurt by his own atomic breath?

Godzilla can be harmed if his own atomic breath is reflected back at him. The Super X2 and the Heisei Mechagodzilla both demonstrated the ability to absorb Godzilla's atomic breath and fire it back at him with several times the force, visibly injuring him.
