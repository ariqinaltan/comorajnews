They Go to Toilet Together: Identical Twin Sisters Dating 40-Year-Old Man Want to Get Pregnant Same

  • Australia's identical twin sisters Anna and Lucy DeCinque are making deliberate efforts to get pregnant at the same time
  • The beautiful ladies dubbed the world's most identical twins do virtually everything together including using the toilet
  • The 35-year-olds took that closeness into their love lives to the extent they have a shared partner who is 40-years-old

Popular identical twin sisters dubbed the world's most identical twins have stated that they want to get pregnant together at exactly the same period.

The Australian ladies identified as Anna and Lucy DeCinque are so close to the point that they do everything together.

The sisters eat, sleep, shower and poo together. They also spend every minute together, wear the same clothes and even have a shared partner, 40-year-old Ben Byrne, Daily Mail reports.
