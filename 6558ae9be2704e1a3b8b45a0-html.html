Skip Bayless says Taylor Swift is fake and doubts she loves Travis Kelce: It seemed a little scripte

Skip Bayless has accused Taylor Swift's relationship with the Kansas City Chiefs' Travis Kelce as being artificial and questions the organic nature of the pair.

Swift and Kelce have been in a relationship since September 2023 but remain very closed-off to the public about their dynamic and the details, although they are slowly becoming more expressive.

The Grammy-winning artist is yet to publicly confirm the couple as real although at this point it's the worst kept "secret" in the world as they've had countless public sightings and declarations of affection.

Whilst the Fox Sports host believes the man's feelings are true and valid, he accuses the woman of possessing ulterior motives and doubts her.

"I sincerely hope her relationship with Travis Kelce is real for both their sakes, but especially for his," he said on The Skip Bayless Show. "I believe, from a distance, that his feelings for her are very real.

"But her feelings for him? I have no idea. How can you know? How can even he be sure? Because she is such a megastar."

Bayless gives his evidence

It's a big claim to make but what is the evidence that the 71-year-old has to prove Swift isn't being her true self?

"When she's up in the [VIP] box [at Arrowhead Stadium], she comes off as stagey and fake to me. I just don't buy it.

"My radar detector is saying, 'Nope nope nope.' I saw that clip of her in her Argentina concert, Travis on his bye week flew down.

"She obviously knew she was on camera. You see her running towards Travis. All that running towards his arms. Embracing him, giving a big exaggerated kiss.

"Big welcome to Argentina. It seemed a little scripted to me."
