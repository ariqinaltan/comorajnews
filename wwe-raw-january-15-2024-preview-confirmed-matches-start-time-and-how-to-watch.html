WWE RAW January 15, 2024 Preview: Confirmed Matches, Start Time and How to Watch

WWE will have a live episode of RAW on January 8th, 2024 and the company is more than determined to knock it out of the park right off the bat. With a loaded show from top to bottom, fans can look forward to a lot of things.

Tonight’s episode of WWE RAW will be broadcast live from the Simmons Bank Arena in North Little Rock, Arkansas. The show will air at 8 PM ET on the USA Network and will feature all the latest builds to the upcoming Royal Rumble Premium Live Event on January 27th.

There are many matches that fans can look forward to. The show is headlined by Seth Rollins defending his WWE World Heavyweight Championship against Jinder Mahal in the main event.

The Miz & R-Truth will be squaring off against Finn Balor & Damian Priest in a tag team bout. #DIY will also be duking it out with DJ McDonagh & Dominik Mysterio. Finally, GUNTHER will make his long-awaited return to the show.

WWE RAW Card for January 15th, 2024 Episode:

  • WWE World Heavyweight Championship: Seth Rollins vs Jinder Mahal
  • The Miz & R-Truth vs. Finn Balor & Damian Priest
  • #DIY vs. DJ McDonagh & Dominik Mysterio
  • GUNTHER Returns

Ringside News will be providing live play-by-play coverage of tonight’s show over in our WWE Monday Night RAW coverage hub. It promises to be a memorable episode for many reasons, so it remains to be seen how the episode will turn out.
