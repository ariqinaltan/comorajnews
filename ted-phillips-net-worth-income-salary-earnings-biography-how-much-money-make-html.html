The NFL Working Club Executive committee and the NFL Senior Club Executive committee, both of which are comprised of NFL players, are responsible for analysing the financial and football operations aspects of the Collective Bargaining Agreement. Phillips is currently one of only two individuals in the NFL to serve on both committees.
