Early Life

In Poway, California, Trevor Moran was born as Trevi Moran. Tim Moran is her father, while Nicole Moran is her mother. Blake is the name of her older brother.

Moran began posting “Apple Store Dances” recordings of herself dancing to popular songs on the Apple Store on YouTube when she was ten.

In August 2012, she established the YouTube channel “Our2ndLife” alongside J.C. Caylen, Connor Franta, Kian Lawley, Ricky Dillon, and Sam Pottorff. Before disbanding in December 2014, the group accumulated three million subscribers.

Career

When she sang “Sexy and I Know It” by LMFAO during her 13-year-old “The X Factor” audition in 2012, Moran first became well-known.

Four judges backed Moran before he was eliminated during the Boot Camp stage. After that, she was selected to participate in the live casting in San Francisco.

In December 2013, she also released “The Dark Side,” which made its Billboard Dance/Electronic Digital Songs chart debut at position 25.

She first released the single “Echo” in June 2014, and then the EP “XIAT,” “Now or Never,” and “Slay” singles.

Moran’s debut EP, “XIAT,” which she released on December 9, 2014, arrived at the top of Billboard’s Top Heatseekers chart.

On October 9, 2015, Moran came out as gay in a YouTube video. She acknowledged having an “identity crisis” the entire year and having second thoughts about her gender identity until realizing she was not transgender at the time in December 2017.
