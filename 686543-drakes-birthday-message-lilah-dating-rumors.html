Drake's Birthday Message Seemingly Confirms Lilah Dating Rumors

Drake's recent Instagram Story has stirred up rumors that he's made things official with his fellow artist, Lilah. He posted a photo of her alongside a sweet happy birthday message. "More life to the gyal that's impossible to duplicate," he begins. He goes on to call her "my inspo my confidant my best mate my heart." Off-and-on rumors that the pair were together began in 2022.

Lilah is also reported to be the woman pictured in the "Search & Rescue" cover art, who fans rumored to be Kim Kardashian earlier this year. Some fans speculated that the cover was an attempted shot at Kardashian's ex-husband, Kanye West, amid their feud. However, it appears that Lilah is much more to Drake than just a Kim K look-alike. Earlier today, J Hus dropped a new track titled, "Who Told You," featuring Drake. Drake also seems to confess his feelings for Lilah in the song, singing, "So Lilah Pi, don't make my eye cry." He also gave fans a taste of the new collab prior to it's release last night.

Drake Wishes Lilah A Happy Birthday

Earlier today, it was also revealed that Drake is nominated for seven BET awards. He has the most nominations of all 2023 nominees, including Album Of The Year, Best Male R&B/Pop Artist, Best Male Hip Hop Artist, and more. Drake's upcoming summer 2023 tour with 21 Savage also begins later this month. The It’s All A Blur tour will include dates all around the U.S., making notable stops at Madison Square Garden, Kia Forum, and more.

One of Drake's other notable relationships, his bromance with Lil Yachty, also went on full display recently. Earlier this week, Drake encouraged Kick to give his friend a streaming deal during a livestream. “Yo, listen! I don’t know who owns Kick, but I’m just saying… You know, you guys, I know the chat wants to see Yachty go live. I’ve been telling him, I feel like he could take over Kick," he told the platform. "There’s a little business idea for you guys, whoever’s responsible for writing the checks. You see the chat’s going crazy for my brother.”
