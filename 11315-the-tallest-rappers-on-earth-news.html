The Tallest Rappers On Earth

"Progress can only come from those rare giants among men, each giant calling to his brother through the desolate intervals of time."

Read on for a breakdown of emcees who could have been ballers. We realize lists like this have already been out, but we decided to get a bit more in depth with it.
