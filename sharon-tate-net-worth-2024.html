Sharon Tate’s Early Life and Career

Sharon Tate was born in Dallas, Texas, and began her career in the entertainment industry as a model. She won the “Miss Richland” beauty pageant in Washington State in 1959, which kickstarted her modeling career. She later moved to Los Angeles to pursue acting and landed her first film role in 1961.

Breakthrough in Hollywood

Tate’s breakthrough role came in 1967 with the film “Valley of the Dolls,” for which she received a Golden Globe nomination. Her performance in the film significantly increased her popularity and earning potential.

Sharon Tate’s Earnings and Net Worth
