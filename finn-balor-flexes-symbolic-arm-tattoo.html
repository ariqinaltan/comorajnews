Finn Balor Flexes Symbolic Arm Tattoo

Finn Balor is a former 2-time NXT Champion and had one of the best runs in NXT during his times there. However, neither of his main roster runs proved to be memorable at all. That said, Balor loves expressing himself as a member of The Judgment Day and it appears he showed off an amazing tattoo recently.

The Judgment Day member is currently one-half of the Undisputed WWE Tag Team Champions after beating Sami Zayn and Kevin Owens for the titles during WWE Payback.

Finn Balor recently visited a tattoo artist and got inked. Balor took to his Instagram and uploaded a couple of photos of his massive arm tattoo, which certainly added to his overall look as a member of The Judgment Day.
