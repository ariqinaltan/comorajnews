Braun Strowman Confirms His Real-Life Girlfriend

Braun Strowman may be the Monster Among Men at home and the big guy with the guns when he’s out shooting with his buddies, but when he goes home, his girlfriend knows him as Adam.

We’ve had suspicions of his relationship for a bit, but this new piece of information confirms that Strowman has a girlfriend waiting for him in his new home inside a gated community.

Kamilla Kaine sent out an Instagram message of herself and her bae Braun. The Monster In The Bank replied saying, ” I miss you babe and will be home soon. Can’t wait to see what you’ve done to the house.”

Our happiest wishes go out to this young couple.
