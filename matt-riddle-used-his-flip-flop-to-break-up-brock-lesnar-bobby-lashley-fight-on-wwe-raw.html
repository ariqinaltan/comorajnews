Matt Riddle Used His Flip-Flop To Break Up Brock Lesnar &amp; Bobby Lashley Fight On WWE RAW

Matt Riddle wasted no time in becoming highlight of WWE television after he finally signed a deal following an impressive stint in the independent circuit. His in-ring skills and chill character work instantly made him beloved as well. He also did something hilarious on RAW this week.

Click here for our complete coverage of WWE RAW this week.

Chaos broke out on WWE Raw as Brock Lesnar and Bobby Lashley met each other on the show. It devolved into an all-out brawl, where even Triple H came out to talk sense into the two.

The whole WWE locker room was present and tried to keep the two apart as well. This included Matt Riddle, who, for some reason, used flip-flops as a way to threaten Lesnar.

Obviously, fans took to this clip and loved Riddle’s contribution. Many also noted how the Original Bro is able to now use his flip-flop as a weapon in dire situations. It certainly made for a funny visual.

We will have to wait and see what WWE has in store for Matt Riddle. By the looks of it, he might be getting paired up with Elias from now on.
