Where is Bryan Okwara from?

The model was born on November 9th, 1985, in Lagos, Nigeria.

Bryan Okwara parents are Igbo from Imo State.

Education

The model studied International Relations part-time at the Lagos State University.

Career

Bryan won the first-ever Mr. Nigeria pageant in 2007 and the Best African Male model in 2010. He is also known as one of the brand ambassadors of OUCH and Martini.

The model also delved into acting, and his first shot at it was in the 2008 movie titled Heart to Heart, which was directed by Remi, the CEO of Africa Awake.
