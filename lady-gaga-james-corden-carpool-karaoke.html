Lady Gaga shows there's no 'Bad Romance' with James Corden on Carpool Karaoke - watch

    Lady Gaga is set to be the latest celebrity to join James Corden for a spot of Carpool Karaoke, and by the look of this new teaser the vivacious singer certainly gives the British show host a commute to remember!

    On Tuesday's episode of The Late Late Show, viewers will get to see the 30-year-old star take a quick trip around Los Angeles singing some of her hit tunes - including Bad Romance.

    They also recreate the moment when Gaga found out she got the call to take centre stage at next year's Super Bowl – where she will headline the infamous halftime show.

    Lady Gaga Carpool Karaoke: Coming Tuesday

    Pretending to be on the phone, James is seen telling the pop star: "Gaga, it’s Dave from the NFL. What are you doing early next year?”

    Looking surprise, the Poker Face hitmaker reacts by saying: "Oh my gosh, are you giving me the Super Bowl?"

    View post on Instagram 

    Earlier this month, Lady Gaga offered her fans her sneak peek into the funny stint, posting a picture with Corden – she wrote in the caption: "Had a lil fun with @JKCorden today! coming soon! #JOANNE #CARPOOLKARAOKE #MILLIONREASONS #PERFECTILLUSION"

    "Best day ever!!!! Can't wait for you all to see it," Corden replied.

    She is the latest in a long list of high-profile stars who have joined James for a spin in his car, including Britney Spears, Adele, Elton John, One Direction, and most surprisingly, First Lady Michelle Obama, who sang along to tracks including Beyoncé's hitSingle Ladies, and Get your Freak On with Missy Elliott.
