Will Ospreay Hints At Opening Forbidden Door To Face Seth Rollins

Will Ospreay is regarded as one of the best pro wrestlers of the last decade, as he has had incredible matches in many promotions in the world. In fact, he even teased facing off against Seth Rollins.

Ospreay is set to defend his IWGP US Title against Shota Umino at NJPW x STARDOM Historic X-Over. As he’s the champion, Ospreay also wants to face some interesting opponents, especially those Behind the Forbidden Door.

Will Ospreay recently told Tokyo Sports that he would love to square off against Seth Rollins in the future. We’ll have to see if he is able to open that Forbidden Door.

I’ve mentioned Seth Rollins’ name many times, and he’s approached me, so I have a lot of thoughts about it,

We will have to wait and see whether The Areal Assassin will face off against The Visionary somewhere down the line or not. Fans would love to see it, and with Triple H in control of WWE’s creative direction, you can really never say never about anything that is best for business.
