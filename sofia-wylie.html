Who is Sofia Wylie?

Career- Actress, Singer, Dancer

Actress Sofia Wylie first began her career as a dancer. She made her first appearance as a dancer in the So You Think You Can Dance reality TV show in 2011. She then tried her hand at singing entering one of the leading platforms of the country called America’s Got Talent in 2015.

In 2016 she got her first role as an actress in the Andi Mack TV series. She then kept on performing as an actor and signed different projects with Disney +.

In 2019 she played the role of Cory Bailey in the movie Back of the Net. In 2022 her movie The School for Good and Evil got released in which she played the lead role of Agatha. This movie of hers was released on 19th October 2022 and has been directed by Paul Feig.
