Why was Jeff Hardy arrested?

Wrestling star Jeff Hardy was arrested on June 13, 2022, on multiple charges.

The former WWE wrestler was arrested in Daytona Beach, Florida.

Why was Jeff Hardy arrested?

Jeff Hardy was arrested in the early hours of Monday morning for driving while intoxicated or under the influence of drugs.

Since it is his third DUI in under ten years, it comes with a third-degree felony charge.

The first two DUIs meant that he had restrictions on his license, and he broke those, which added to his rap sheet.

Hardy was also charged for driving with a suspended, canceled, or revoked license.

When were Hardy's previous arrests?

Hardy was previously arrested in March 2018 in Concord, North Carolina, for driving while impaired.

Most recently, he was arrested in Moore County, North Carolina, for his second DUI, in October 2019.
