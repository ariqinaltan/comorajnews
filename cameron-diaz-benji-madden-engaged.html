Cameron Diaz and Benji Madden 'engaged after seven months'

    Cameron Diaz and Benji Madden are engaged, according to new reports. After weeks of speculation that the couple have taken their relationship to the next level, insiders close to the pair have now confirmed that Benji, 35, has popped the question. The Good Charlotte rocker and his Hollywood love, 42, first stepped out together in May, fuelling rumours that they were quietly dating. After the pair were seen cosying up to each other in public, Cameron and Benji confirmed that they were very much an item.

    Benji Madden and Cameron Diaz have been together since May

    Seven months on and it looks like the lovebirds are set to marry, with a wedding reported to be planned for early 2015. "Everyone thinks it's wild but are so happy for them," one source told . "Benji always tends to fall in love easily, but this time it's for real and he landed a great girl. They obviously both make each other incredibly happy and there's nothing better than that." Back in September, a source said that Benji was keen to go down the traditional route of getting married first and then think about starting a family. "She really doesn't care about getting married but wants babies," they said. "He wants to get engaged and married first."

Nicole is married to Joel Madden, Benji's twin brother and bandmate. "I'm going to take responsibility for everything," Nicole said back in July on .

    Cameron Diaz is said to be marrying early next year

Cameron has been keeping fans guessing on the status of her relationship. Since October, the Charlie's Angels actress has been spotted wearing a diamond-encrusted ring on her left hand, fuelling rumours that she said "Yes" to Benji.
