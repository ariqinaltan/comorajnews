Dennis Rodman’s Basketball Career

Rodman’s basketball career was nothing short of extraordinary. He was known for his fierce defensive and rebounding abilities, earning him the nickname “The Worm”. He won five NBA championships and was inducted into the Basketball Hall of Fame in 2011.

Income from Basketball

Post-Basketball Career

After retiring from basketball, Rodman ventured into various fields, including acting, wrestling, and reality TV, which contributed to his net worth.

Acting and Wrestling

Rodman made appearances in several films and TV shows, including “Double Team” and “The Apprentice”. He also had a brief stint in professional wrestling with World Championship Wrestling (WCW), where he reportedly earned $1.5 million.

Reality TV and Endorsements

Financial Troubles

Despite his earnings, Rodman faced significant financial troubles. His extravagant lifestyle, coupled with legal issues and child support payments, led to a decline in his net worth.

Legal Issues and Child Support

Lavish Lifestyle
