Beyonce &quot;Self-Titled&quot; Documentary

Beyonce drops off the third segment in her "Self-Titled" documentary series. In this part she discusses all the 17 music videos that went with her visual album, and the people who made it happen. Some of the video directors also chime in on what Bey is like to work with.

Experience Beyonce "Self-Titled" Documentary, a new offering from Beyonce, which was released on Wednesday, December 25th, 2013. Things are looking better for Beyonce with each new release, which Beyonce "Self-Titled" Documentary has proven yet again. It's worthy addition to the impressive body of work Beyonce has been developing over the years. We're looking forward to hearing what's next.
