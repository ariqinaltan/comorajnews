Good Brothers Win Impact Wrestling Tag Team Titles

Impact Wrestling presented Slammiversary tonight and there were a few big moments. Karl Anderson and Doc Gallows intended on walking out of the building as Tag Team Champions, and that is exactly what they accomplished.

Click here for our complete coverage of Impact Wrestling’s Slammiversary pay-per-view.

Violent By Design, the incoming champions, had the match won on two occasions, but the Good Brothers stopped them. In the end, Gallows and Anderson nailed a Magic Killer and picked up the gold.

This marks Gallows and Anderson’s second reign as Impact Wrestling World Tag Team Champions. This was a controversial victory, and Violent By Design was not happy about things. Now the Good Brothers are champions in Impact Wrestling once again.
