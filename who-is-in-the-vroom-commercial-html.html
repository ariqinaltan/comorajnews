What is the purpose of Cosentyx?

Known as an immunosuppressant, Cosentyx (secukinumab) works by reducing the effects of a chemical molecule in the body that has the potential to trigger inflammation. Among adults, Cosentyx is used for the treatment of mild-to-moderate plaque psoriasis (raised, silvery flaking of the skin), ankylosing spondylitis, and psoriatic arthritis, among other conditions.
