Dan Sur Says He's The First Rapper To Get Gold Chains Implanted Into Their Scalp

Mexican rapper Dan Sur says he is the “first rapper … in human history” to have gold chains implanted directly onto his head. The new implants hang from his scalp similar to hair.

“The truth is that I wanted to do something different because I see that everyone dyes their hair. I hope not everyone copies me now,” he said in a clip posted to his TikTok account, which boasts 1.9 million followers.

The 23-year-old claims he had the surgery done back in April.

In another viral TikTok, he explains: “I have it as a hook that is implanted in my head and that hook has hooks and they are all hooked in my skull, under my skin. This is my hair. Golden hair. The first rapper to have gold hair implanted in human history.”

“I’m feeling good. I still have the diamond so I feel good," Uzi recently told TMZ.
