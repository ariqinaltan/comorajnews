What Time Does The Shops at Montebello CA Close and Open?

The Shops at Montebello CA is one of the best shopping experiences in the Montebello area, conveniently located at 2134 Montebello Town Center Dr, Montebello, CA 90640. Is The Shops at Montebello CA Open on Saturday and Sunday?
