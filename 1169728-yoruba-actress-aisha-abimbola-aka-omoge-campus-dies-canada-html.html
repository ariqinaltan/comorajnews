Yoruba actress Aisha Abimbola aka Omoge Campus dies in Canada

Famous Nigerian actress Aisha Abimbola popularly known as Omoge Campus is dead. She died on Tuesday, May 15.

The sudden death of the actress was broken by her colleague, Bisola Badmus, who shared her picture on Instagram. Badmus’ first post reads: “Unquestionably God RIP Omoge Campus.”

On Wednesday, May 16, she shared the picture of the deceased with the caption: “Gone so soon RIP omogecampus.”

According to Goldmyne TV, it was reported that the deceased allegedly died of breast cancer in Canada. Late Aisha Abimbola was born on December 19, in Epe, Lagos lsland, Lagos state. She studied Hotel Management from Lagos state Polytechnic (LASPOTECH).
